About Riccio Studio

Marketing that respects
your time and money.

We're a specialist marketing studio working exclusively with local service businesses. We care about one thing: results you can see in your revenue.

Our Story

Built by someone who got frustrated with bad marketing advice.

I started Riccio Studio after spending years watching great local businesses waste money on marketing that never actually worked — vanity metrics, confusing reports, and agencies who talked a lot but delivered very little.

Local businesses deserve better. A restaurant owner shouldn't need a marketing degree to understand what they're paying for. A salon owner shouldn't be presented with a 40-page report when all they want to know is "did we get more bookings?"

So we built a studio around one principle: plain language, clear goals, honest results. We work with a small number of clients so we can genuinely focus on each one. And we only measure success by the things that matter to you — customers, bookings and revenue.

Our values

The four things we never compromise on.

Honesty

We tell you what's working and what isn't — even when it's uncomfortable. No hiding behind vanity metrics or confusing jargon.

Results-first

Likes and impressions are nice. But we're obsessed with the numbers that actually matter: customers, bookings and revenue.

Approachable

You can always reach us. You'll always understand what we're doing. We keep things simple and communicate like real people.

Calm & Reassuring

Running a business is stressful enough. When you work with us, marketing stops being something to worry about and becomes something that just works.

FAQs

Common questions.

How much does it cost to work with Riccio Studio? +
It depends on the services you need and the scope of work. We offer monthly retainers starting from a level that works for small local businesses, and we're always transparent about what's included. We'll give you a clear proposal after the free audit call — no surprises.
How quickly will I see results? +
Some things — like Google Ads and reputation management — can show results within weeks. SEO and organic social typically take 3–6 months to build meaningful momentum. We'll be honest with you about realistic timelines from the very first conversation.
Do I need to sign a long contract? +
We typically work on a rolling monthly basis after an initial 3-month period to allow strategies to take root. We believe in earning your business every month — not locking you in when results aren't there.
How involved do I need to be? +
As involved as you want to be. Some clients like to be closely involved in content approval and strategy. Others hand us the keys and trust us to get on with it. We adapt to what works best for you. Most clients spend no more than 1–2 hours per month on marketing-related tasks with us.
Do you work with businesses outside the UK? +
Our focus is on UK-based local businesses. If you're based outside the UK, get in touch and we'll let you know honestly if we're a good fit for your situation.
